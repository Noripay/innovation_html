THE CHALLENGE

Malaysia's Logistics Bottleneck

In Malaysia, "Last-Mile Delivery" accounts for nearly 53% of total shipping costs. With e-commerce revenue surging, our urban centers are struggling with a "Gridlock Crisis" that hampers growth.

  • Economic Impact: Traffic costs Malaysia an estimated RM20 Billion annually.
  • Shipping Barriers: Over 51% of Malaysian sellers identify shipping as their biggest operational challenge.
